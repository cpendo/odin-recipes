Baked Tortilla Chips

Description

Tasty tortilla chips baked at home with corn tortillas are much better than store-bought chips. With an amazing lime and cumin flavor, these crispy, golden chips taste great with your favorite salsa, guacamole, or hummus.

Ingredients

  • corn tortillas
  • lime juice
  • vegetable oil
  • spices: salt, cumin, chilli powder

Steps

  1. Cut the tortillas into triangles â€” each tortilla should have at least 8 chips.
  2. Add lime juice and oil to a spray bottle and spray each chip.
  3. Sprinkle each chip with the spices.
  4. Bake the chips, flipping and re-seasoning halfway through.
  5. Make sure to check them often so they don't burn.
